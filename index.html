"La La Land," directed by Damien Chazelle, is a 2016 musical film that pays homage to classic Hollywood musicals. Set in the vibrant landscapes of Los Angeles, the story follows Mia, an aspiring actress, and Sebastian, a jazz musician, as they navigate the challenges of pursuing their dreams. It is no doubt that this is one of the best films ever made, winning 112 awards, having a rotten tomatoes rating of 91% and still be watched and talked about by many people.

The Film

Two very different people meet eachother, an aspiring actress and a jazz musician, as they navigate the challenges of pursuing their dreams in Los Angeles. The two fall in love together, but as they strive for succes in their respective career, they find themselves facing the inevitable tensions between love and ambition.

The Characters

Mia Dolan

Mia, portrayed by Emma Stone in 'La La Land', is an aspiring actress navigating the competitive landscape of Hollywood. Her character is defined by a blend of determination, vulnerability, and a passion for her dreams, making her a central figure in the film's exploration of love and ambition.

Sebastian Wilder

Sebastian, played by Ryan Gosling in 'La La Land', is a jazz musician with a deep love for the art form and a dream of opening his own jazz club. His character is characterized by a blend of passion for music, a hint of nostalgia for the past, and a commitment to his artistic principles, which adds depth to the film's narrative.

Symbolisms

From the vibrant coloura of Mia's dress to the recurring motif of jazz and the changing seasons, the film has loads of symbolisms, inviting the audience to delve beyond the surface.
